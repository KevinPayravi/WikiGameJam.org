October 3-5 • Hex House in Brooklyn

Join developers, designers, and Wikipedia enthusiasts for a weekend of creative hacking, October 3-5. We're building games and interactive experiences powered by the world's largest encyclopedia.

Turn Knowledge Into Play

Build Something Amazing

Want to build a browser game that uses Wikipedia's live edit feed? Or a trivia app powered by Wikidata? Or something completely wild we haven't thought of yet? This is your weekend to make it happen.

All Are Welcome

Solo hacker or part of a team? Complete beginner or seasoned pro? Doesn't matter – bring your ideas and we'll figure out the rest together.

Bring Your Curiosity

Three days of collaborative learning, brainstorming, and hacking with developers, designers, and Wikipedians who love turning serious encyclopedic knowledge into playful experiences.

Win & Connect Globally

The winning project will receive a stipend to attend a future Wikimedia hackathon or conference, connecting you with the global community of knowledge builders.
